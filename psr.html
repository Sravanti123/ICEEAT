Submission Methods

1) Proceedings of ICEAT 2023 will be published with ISBN number.All papers of ICEAT 2023 which is selected for presentation will be published in Google Scholar, SCIE, Scopus, Web Of Science, Anna University Annexure 1 with additional fee.

2) Full text papers (.pdf and .doc) will be accepted by Email- Iceeat2k23conference@gmail.com

3)MEET / VIDEO presenatation also available.

*Note* The conference secretary office will contact you within 3 work days when your submission is well received to inform you the paper ID as well as other feedbacks.

Please contact us if you failed to receive feedbacks from the conference secretary when you submitted your paper 3 work days later.

Policy on Dual Submission

Submissions that are identical (or substantially similar) to versions that have been previously published, or accepted for publication, or that have been submitted in parallel to other conferences or journals are not appropriate for ICEAT and violate our dual submission policy.
